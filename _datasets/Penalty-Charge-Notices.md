notes: **[Decriminalised Parking Enforcement (DPE)](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/1991/40/contents) enables local authorities in Scotland to administer its own parking penalties, including the issuing of Penalty Charge Notices (PCNs) to vehicles who breach parking controls in specified areas.**

**This dataset lists all PCNs issued and the contravention (reason) for the issue since DPE began on 03/05/2017.**
